# Agent recipes — daily prompts for your Growth OS

Once the AppFlowy MCP is connected, Claude can run these. They map to the
Growth OS databases (Library, Lessons, Notes, Papers, Repos, Signals, Sources,
Review, Mission Intake).

## Morning (2 min)
- "Give me today's brief: new Papers/Repos/Signals from the Inbox, lessons due
  for review, and my next unread Library essential. Keep it tight."
- "Triage the Papers inbox: mark anything off-topic Archived, and tag the 3
  most relevant to my work as Reading."

## Turning input into durable learning
- "I just finished <book/paper>. Pull 3–5 atomic Lessons from it into the
  Lessons DB (Domain, one-line principle, Detail), status Capture, NextReview
  tomorrow."
- "Summarize this repo's README into the repo's Why field and set Status Trying."

## Notes & work
- "Create a Note titled '<topic>' linking the related Lessons and Papers."
- "From my Notes tagged #forecasting, draft a short synthesis of what I've learned."
- "Draft a Mission Intake card in Section=Betts Basketball for <specific repo
  update>, Risk=L2, Priority=P1/P2, with clear acceptance criteria. Leave it
  in Backlog so I can approve it on the board."
- "Draft a Mission Intake card in Section=DAGs for <pipeline issue>, Risk=L2,
  Priority=P1/P2, and include the target DAG/output asset. Leave it in Backlog."
- "Draft a Mission Intake card in Section=Learning for <topic>, Risk=L1,
  Priority=P2, asking for a plan or synthesis instead of code changes."

## Weekly review (10 min, Sundays)
- "Weekly review: count what I finished in Library/Papers/Repos this week,
  list Lessons I marked Internalized, and surface 5 Signals I saved but didn't act on."
- "Suggest next week's focus from my open Reading items and saved Repos."

## Maintenance
- "Promote Lessons I rated Confidence 5 to Internalized and push their NextReview out."
- "Find duplicate Papers (same ArxivID) and archive the older row."
